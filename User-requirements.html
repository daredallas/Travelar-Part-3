User Requirements

Create opportunities for businesses to connect with potential customers:

Business owners can add their businesses to the app and provide information to users. They are also able of adding tags to associate with other similar businesses. The prototype does not support the ability for creating a business profile but it does illustrate how there will be the ability to create a custom page.

Provide recommendations and alternatives to immediate location:

Each business can contain tags, provided by either user or business owner. The prototype demonstrates that tapping on a specific tag will display places with the same tag. The prototype does not currently support users adding tags, but it does show how tapping a tag will show matching results.

Connecting users to the locations around them through the app, presenting landmark locations with special interface and information:

The prototype display more than one location at once but has a maximum so that only the most popular destinations show up. DIfferent colours are used to categorize landmarks and attractions. For example, landmarks are displayed as blue bubbles Labels can be tapped to lead to a detailed view with more information on the site.
